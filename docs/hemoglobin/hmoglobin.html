The Narrative

Planet Hemoglobin is coming out of a 500 year long ice-age due to intense factory production using nuclear energy and greenhouse gases which are melting the ice, as well as pumping enormous amounts of toxins and chemicals into the atmosphere creating a whole new series of never before seen cataclysmic events and “natural” disasters.

This would prove to be a very big problem, as the beings of this planet have already gotten acclimated to cold weather. To make matters worse, the beings on this planet have no sense of touch or sensitivity to physical stimuli other than vision and hearing. This makes anticipating and understanding the effects of new environmental disasters nearly impossible.

The climate / weather of this planet is variable but the mood is ominous and the air is filled with fear of the unknown. The skies are a mix of dark and toxin filled clouds and hues. The environment is full of thick dense fog and the melting toxin-filled ice glaciers add to the eeriness of the planet. To protect against these elements now and in the past (with the ice age) the species on this planet (who are essentially only skeletons) cover themselves in pumpkins as an adoptive exoskeleton, similar to how hermit crabs use shells as protection.

The society is a dystopian pig farming society in which pigs are the only commodity, wealth, and food supply. Our characters survive solely on extracting and ingesting the blood of the pig. The purest blood is the red blood cells in the heart of the pigs() a mere 3-4 drops per pig). Purness of blood dictste value: Whole blood is the least valuable (cluttered with unnecessary platelets, plasma etc), next is blood without plasma, then blood without platelets, then blood that is just red and white blood cells, and then blood that is just red blood cells.

The story follows a slavedriver dictator who desires nothing more than the purest pig blood at the expense of her people. She has a massive factory that farms these rare blood cells. She needs to farm roughly 1000 pigs every days just for her personal use. The deconstruction of the atmosphere comes not only from the industrialization pig-blood extraction and shipping, but also the toxicity of the pig bodies themselves which are irresponsibly thrown into local bodies of water; creating magmic lakes of toxic energy.
