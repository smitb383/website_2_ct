Dear Morgan

Data-Driven-Website

After discovering I would not be able to talk to my sister, whom I am very close to, for several months, I created a data driven template that would allow me to thnk about and feel connected to her while she was gone. Forever daunted by the periless task of catching friends and family up on months of my life, I created this template to make a organized journal account of my life and the outside world while my sister is away. Without the necessity of signifigant life events to occur in during her absence, in this way I can provide my sister with a realistic account of what my life was like while she was gone; using the lanuguage and causal sisterly format we normally communicate with. Pulling from a google sheet using JSON, this page dynamically updates when I write to the google sheet, to make it easy and code-free in the everyday to keep up journaling constancy. Additionally this meditative practice will enable me to talk to my sister everyday and feel close to her while she is gone.
