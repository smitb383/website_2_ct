Soundscape

Connecting Location to Music History via AR

Soundscape is an interactive game that maps songs to signifigant geographic locations in New York via Augmented Reality and music. The game will involve multiple users interacting in the same space, at the same time, and can always join into the same augmented world. The users will be looking for “musical checkpoints” that they are directed to by an AR visual through their mobile devices. The user is led to the target and then given other information about the song. While the game has no beginning or end, its larger purpose is to promote connectivity to the physical world through technology , employing easily accessible and open source augmented reality applications as a tool to connect people to author tangible and geographic social connectivity.

The aim of soundscape is to encourage hisotrical and musical learning and enjoyment remind users of the creative power of, the sometimes defeating, New York by highlighting its inspiring musical geography.

Soundscape brings the participant into the real world urban environment and functions at specific sites that will appear in the augmented reality of the game. The augmented scope will be based on geographic location. Users can activate these objects to access the music that is infused with them. We aim to root music in hisorical location to provide a type of emotional connection to space and the unveiling often overlooked or undiscovered histories of the place in the city that it appears in. The music that appears in each specific spot will correlate with a moment in history, history of an artist, or a cinematic moment in history from that space. The database of music and historical location would be an open source library that we would invite participants to add to to create a encompassing and inclusive soundscape of New York.
